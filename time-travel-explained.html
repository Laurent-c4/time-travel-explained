Time Travel Explained

Is time travel even possible?

This is a topic that many would not dare talk about. Would you believe me if I told you it is, in theory, "possible" to travel into the future? Sounds crazy huh!

You will have to take note of the following before I explain this interesting fact to you:

  • Time
  • Mass
  • Speed

Theoretical explanation about time travel into the future

First of all you would have to travel at "almost" the speed of light since it is impractical to travel at the speed of light due to the equation of mass- energy equivalence. Here comes the interesting part. If you were able to travel at, say 90% the speed of light, then you would experience a slowing in time. For every ten minutes you experience, a stationery person will experience twenty minutes! Your time will be cut into half!
Therefore when you finally come to a stop you will have in a way travelled into the future! Imagine spending ten years travelling at 90% the speed of light. The rest of the people will have aged twenty years, ten more than you! Time travel into the future, right? Haha!

Three things though:

  1. Where will you find a vessel capable of propelling you that fast?
  2. Where will you be going to if at all you get that vessel?
  3. Will you ever come back?

They say anything is posssible, right? Well let's wait and see if this will ever be achieved. Maybe our great, great grandchildren will... haha!
